Pasta

Recipe

Boil Water: Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil. Add a generous amount of salt (about 1-2 tablespoons) to season the pasta. Add Pasta: Once the water is boiling, add the pasta and stir immediately to prevent it from sticking. Cook according to the package instructions, usually 8-12 minutes, until it's al dente (firm to the bite). Drain Pasta: When the pasta is cooked, drain it in a colander, but reserve a cup of the pasta water in case you need it to adjust the sauce's consistency. Add Sauce and Serve: Return the pasta to the pot and toss with your desired sauce. Use a bit of reserved pasta water to loosen the sauce if needed. Serve hot and enjoy!
